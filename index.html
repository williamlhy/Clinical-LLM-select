Research

LLMs review

We have collected and documented 330 large language models (LLMs) in the medical domain to accomplish various types of clinical tasks and basic information about the LLMs. By considering a five-stage clinical workflow, we provide some recommendations for the clinical integration of LLMs. Emphasis is placed on our development of a clinical LLM selection tool, which is designed to provide evidence-based guidance to medical practitioners in identifying the LLMs that are best suited to their specific needs.

CDS in ZJU

Center for Data Science (CDS) in Zhejiang University (ZJU) is a university-level academic innovation research institution focusing on the theory and application of big data and talent training with statistics, applied mathematics, computer science and management as the core supporting disciplines. CDS is committed to facing the strategic forward-looking issues and social needs of big data, building a high quality and efficiency innovation platform for core technology and theoretical research in data science.
